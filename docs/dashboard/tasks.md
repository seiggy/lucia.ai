# Tasks

The Tasks page lets you track and manage tasks created by Lucia during conversations.

![Tasks](/img/dashboard/tasks.png)

## Status Counters

At the top of the page, counters summarize tasks by status: **pending**, **in progress**, **completed**, and **failed**. Click a counter to filter the list to that status.

## Active Tasks

The **Active Tasks** view shows all currently pending and in-progress tasks. Each task card displays its title, assigned agent, creation time, and current status. Click a task to view its full details including the originating conversation and any subtasks.

## Task History

Switch to the **Task History** tab to view completed and archived tasks. This view includes the same detail but also shows completion time and outcome. Tasks can be searched by keyword or filtered by date range.

## Task Lifecycle

Tasks are created automatically when agents identify actionable items during conversations. They progress through statuses as agents work on them and are archived once completed or manually dismissed.
